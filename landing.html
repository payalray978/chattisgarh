About

The Department of Commerce and Industries is Nodal Department for overall development of industrial sector in the state.

The department plays developmental & facilitation role to attract industrial investments in the state. It focuses on creating an industry friendly environment and formulating suitable policies in the state aimed at propelling fast pace modernisation & strengthening of the industrial units.
